// Powerups

Buy from the merchant between floors. Stack them. Break the game.

Iron Skin

+2 max HP per stack (heals immediately). Up to ×5.

Swift Boots

+12% movement speed (multiplicative). Up to ×5.

Armor

Take 1 less damage per hit (max 3 reduction). Up to ×3.

Keen Eye

+12% critical hit chance. Up to ×10.

Brutal Force

+0.4× critical damage multiplier. Up to ×10.

Vampirism

Heal 1 HP on every kill. Up to ×3.

Sharpness

+1 flat damage to all attacks. Up to ×5.

Haste

−10% attack cooldown (multiplicative). Up to ×5.

Gold Fever

+75% coin drops. Up to ×3.

Berserker

+25% damage when below half HP. Up to ×5.
